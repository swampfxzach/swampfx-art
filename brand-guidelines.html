01 / Brand Overview

BORN FROM
THE DARK.

Swamp FX creates handcrafted horror and special effects prosthetic masks for haunt professionals, Halloween enthusiasts, cosplay artists, and film production.

Every piece is built from a tradition of practical effects artistry — no digital shortcuts, no factory floors. Just the craft, the material, and the vision.

The brand carries the energy of underground horror culture: raw, obsessive, authentically strange.

02 / Primary Brand Mark

THE MASCOT

Primary — On Black
Light Background Variant
Red Eye Variant
Atmospheric Context

Character Anatomy

  • Acid-green rotting zombie flesh — the signature brand color made physical
  • Exposed ribcage with root & chain entanglement — decay and captivity
  • Reaching clawed hand — the brand's grasp motif; hunger, emergence
  • Rising from earth and swamp — undead rebirth; the thing that won't stay buried

Original ink study, Nov 2021 — the character's origin.

The Origin

The Swamp FX mascot began as a hand-inked illustration — every line a deliberate act of craft. From raw ink to full color, the character evolved through the same hands-on process that defines every mask the brand creates. The origin art is part of the brand's DNA: proof that it started from nothing but skill, patience, and a love of horror.

Atmospheric backgrounds. Apparel substrate color.

Slate Gray
#5C6470 RGB 92 / 100 / 112

Ground textures, muted UI elements, secondary text.

Blood Red
#AA0000 RGB 170 / 0 / 0

Red Eye accent only. Maximum one use per composition.

⚠

Blood Red — Handle with Care. This color is reserved exclusively for the glowing red eye variant of the mascot. Use a maximum of one instance per composition. Never substitute Blood Red for Toxic Slime Green as a primary brand color.

Voice Pillars

01
CRAFT FIRST

Technique, materials, and process are the story. Show the work. Describe the foam latex, name the pigments, reveal the hours. The method is the mystique.

02
HORROR IS CULTURE

Speak to enthusiasts as peers, not consumers. Horror fans are knowledgeable, passionate, and allergic to condescension. Match their depth.

03
MAKE IT VISCERAL

Language should have texture. Make them feel the latex, smell the paint, hear the mold release. If your copy doesn't conjure sensation, it isn't doing its job.

07 / Usage Guidelines

USAGE RULES

DO

Use the text mark on black, white, or forest-green backgrounds only. These three substrates are the only approved contexts for the wordmark.

DO

Maintain minimum clear space — 1× the cap height of the "X" in FX — on all sides of the mark at all times.

DO

Use the complete lockup (mascot + wordmark) when both elements can be displayed at legible scale. Default to the combined mark when space allows.

DO

Reproduce in full Toxic Slime Green (#5AC800) or solid white for single-color print applications. Both are approved single-color versions.

DON'T

Stretch or distort the logo on any axis. Proportions are locked. Use only the original files — never recreate the letterforms manually.

DON'T

Place the mark on busy photographic backgrounds where the acid-green fill is lost against competing tones. Always check contrast first.

DON'T

Add additional outlines, drop shadows, glows, or stroke effects to the logo. The letterforms already carry built-in spike geometry — extra effects become noise.

DON'T

Substitute Blood Red (#AA0000) for Toxic Slime Green as the primary logo color. Blood Red is reserved exclusively for the Red Eye mascot variant.
