03 / FAQ

COMMON
QUESTIONS

How long does a custom mask take? ▼

Most custom masks take 4–8 weeks depending on complexity and materials. Highly detailed builds or full-head silicone pieces may run longer. Rush orders may be available — ask Zach when you reach out.

What materials do you use? ▼

Swamp FX masks are built with professional-grade foam latex and platinum silicone — the same materials used in Hollywood production. All paints are skin-safe and tested for extended wear.

Can I wear the mask for extended periods? ▼

Yes. Comfort and wearability are part of the design process from day one. Zach accounts for breathability, vision range, and fit during the sculpt phase so the finished piece actually works for its intended use.

Do you ship? ▼

Yes — Swamp FX ships nationwide within the US. Each mask is packed carefully to protect the finish during transit. International shipping is available on request — reach out for a quote.

Can I send reference images? ▼

Absolutely — and the more the better. The more visual reference you can provide, the better Zach can nail the character. Include refs in your project brief or email them directly to Cavemanoutdoors@gmail.com.
